2. What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?

The DNS pairs ip addresses with domain names. 
People search domain names inside a browser, that domain name gets sent from the browser to the DNS, then the DNS finds the ip address that corresponds to that domain name. 

3. What is a cookie?

Cookies are text files that web servers store on your computer to identify you as a specific user.
The data stored on cookies allow the sites to cater more specifically from user to user.
